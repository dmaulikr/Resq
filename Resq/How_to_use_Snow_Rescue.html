Snow Rescue is meant as an extra safety feature at ski resorts. We know you would rather be ripping up the fresh powder than trying to figure out how to use an app. That’s why we made Snow Rescue as simple as possible. Here’s now to use Snow Rescue in 3 easy steps:


  1. Add your skiing/snowboarding buddies to the Buddy List. This is done on the Settings page, either by selecting the “Close by”, or “Contacts” option. The people you add will appear on your Buddy List.

  2. Set the timer. Simply set the timer on the Settings page by using the slider bar.

  3. Swipe over to the Activate page and hit the big button to activate. Snow Rescue is now active and you can exit the app and lock your screen. The app will keep running in the background until you deactivate it, or it sends an alert to the people on your Buddy List.


If you should stop moving while skiing/snowboarding, by falling into a tree well or otherwise being immobilized, Snow Rescue can alert your buddies almost immediately. You’ll hopefully never have to need this app, but if you do, you’ll be happy to have it!
